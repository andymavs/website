Learn & Live Security

University at Buffalo Network Defense is a Cyber Security class & club

LEARN

Whether you're already a kali wiz or don't know what kali is, learn marketable skills in a growing field. Working in a colaborative environment, everyone is welcome and it's never to late to start.

BUILD

Take what you learn past concepts. Design and build your very own full scale network to give practical meaning to the knowlege that you aquire.

COMPETE

Step up and take your skills to the next level. Join a UBNetDef team and travel to competitions, where you can go head to head with top schools to compete for fame and prizes.
